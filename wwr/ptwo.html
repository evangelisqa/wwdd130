Inspiring Messages

Hope

Hope is a source of joy in our lives. Hope helps us endure through trials. We develop hope gradually, like learning to walk, one step at a time. We increase our capacity to “abound in hope, through the power of the Holy Ghost,” as we live the gospel more faithfully

Faith

Faith is more than just believing. You show your faith by how you live. The Savior said, “If ye will have faith in me ye shall have power to do whatsoever thing is expedient in me” (Moroni 7:33). Faith in Jesus Christ can inspire you to follow His perfect example (see John 14:12).

Gratitude

We have many blessings in our lives. We can uplift ourselves and others by choosing to be positive and grateful. If ingratitude is a serious sin, then gratitude is a noble virtue. We can cultivate a grateful heart within us by refusing to dwell on negative thoughts.

Love

We are all spirit children of God before we come to earth. God is the source of all love, so we have the ability and the need to love and to be loved. One of the things we remember most from our premortal life is how much we loved our Father and Jesus and how much They loved us.
