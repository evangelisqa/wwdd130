About Me

Evan

Howdy ya'll Let me introduce myself. My name is Evangelis Albesa. I'm from Vancouver, British Columbia but I'm originally from the Philippines.

I came to Canada in 2012. I came from a big family with nine siblings together. I'm the second from the youngest.

I have been a convert member since 2008 and I joined the church when I was working in Taiwan. I’m so grateful for my membership of the church it helps

me to improve myself to be a good disciple of Jesus Christ. It helps me to grow and learn about why I'm here in this mortal life. My favourite place to

be is in the temple. After I was endowed in 2015 I decided to serve the temple and I feel joy every time I visit the House of the Lord.

I enjoy doing a lot of things. I'm excited getting to know all of you. Thank you for reading my short story.
